cond-hand suits pitching a new life and future in construction without a single clue ab-
out how one was going to build it - I mean this is bigger a glorified b2b ad agency.

and technology, in other words earnest hacking is my true calling.

That said, I have decided to open-source all my Primebuild material for free public use,
including some of my sourced drawings, new-builds, and even my first campaign as a
novice back in the day.
