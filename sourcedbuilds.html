Sourced Builds

Primebuild at one point was my life force, calling - recruiting everything slowly at once,
Now it seems to have been a little bit flattering to go around my local town with my second-hand
suits pitching a new life and future in construction without a single clue about how one was going to build it -
I mean this is bigger a glorified b2b ad agency.

2025 has been the year of re-evaluation, and I have come to the conclusion that science
and technology, in other words earnest hacking is my true calling.

That said, I have decided to open-source all my Primebuild material for free public use, including
some of my sourced drawings, new-builds, and even my first campaign as a novice back in the day.
